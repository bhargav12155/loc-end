How we handle your location data and personal information

Important: This application collects location data for tracking and monitoring purposes. By using this service, you consent to the collection and processing of your location information as described below.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

  • GPS Coordinates: Precise latitude and longitude from your device
  • Timestamp: When location data was collected
  • Session Information: Unique session identifiers for tracking visits
  • Geofence Events: Entry and exit events from predefined areas

Feedback Information

  • Your responses to feedback questions
  • Optional contact information (name, email, phone) if provided
  • Timestamp of feedback submission

Technical Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (may be collected by server)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Location Monitoring: Tracking movement within designated areas
  • Geofence Alerts: Notifying when you enter or leave specific locations
  • Feedback Analysis: Understanding visitor experiences and preferences
  • Service Improvement: Enhancing the application functionality
  • Communication: Contacting you if you've provided contact information

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • Stored on secure servers with appropriate safeguards
  • Retained only as long as necessary for the stated purposes
  • Protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure
Note: Location data is collected continuously while the application is active in your browser. You can stop location tracking by closing the application or denying location permissions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only:

  • With authorized personnel who need access for legitimate business purposes
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

6. Location Services

This application requires location services to function properly. You can:

  • Grant or deny location permissions through your browser settings
  • Stop location tracking by closing the application
  • Clear stored location data through your browser's storage settings

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser storage to:

  • Store application preferences
  • Cache location data for offline functionality
  • Maintain session information
  • Improve application performance
